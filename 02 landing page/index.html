Random bird facts.

There are around 10,000 different species of bird. They range from big to small, and are lots of different colours!

All female birds lay eggs. Their baby birds then hatch out of the shells when they are ready. If you look up into the trees, you might be able to see a nest.

A lot of birds migrate which means, depending on the season, they fly elsewhere. This is often to do with the weather of different countries or places.

All birds have feathers and they are very useful for lots of different reasons. Feathers help birds to fly. They also keep birds warm in the winter!
