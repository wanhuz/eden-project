Classic Cheesecake Recipe

Descriptions

Look no further for a creamy and ultra smooth classic cheesecake recipe! Paired with a buttery graham cracker crust, no one can deny its simple decadence. For the best results, bake in a water bath.

Ingredients

  • Block cream cheese: Four 8-ounce blocks of full-fat cream cheese
  • Sugar: 1 cup.
  • Sour cream: 1 cup.
  • 1 cup Mushrooms diced
  • A little flavor: 1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
  • Eggs: 3 eggs

Directions

  1. And as always, make sure all of the cheesecake batter ingredients are at room temperature so the batter remains smooth, even, and combines quickly.
  2. Beating cold ingredients together will result in a chunky over-beaten cheesecake batter, hardly the way we want to start!
